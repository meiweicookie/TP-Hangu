Discover Amazing places in Korea

South Korea is the name usually given to a beautiful, prosperous and distinctive country located on the eastern edge of Asia, in the southern part of the Korean Peninsula. The official name of the state is the Republic of Korea. Korea boasts an outstanding history, rich culture and stunning nature. Its shores are washed by the waters of three seas included in the Pacific Ocean - the Yellow, the Japanese and the Southern, as the Koreans themselves call the Korea Strait.
